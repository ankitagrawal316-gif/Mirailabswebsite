01
AI-Powered Month-End Close Automation
From 10 Days to 2–3 Days
THE OLD WAY

The monthly close is a fire drill every month — reconciliations, journal entries, accruals, intercompany eliminations, flux analysis — all manual across spreadsheets. It takes 5–10 business days. Teams are so consumed closing the books they have zero time for analysis, forecasting, or strategic partnership.

→
WITH MIRAI

AI automates the close end-to-end: auto-reconciles accounts, generates recurring journal entries, calculates accruals from historical patterns, flags exceptions, and produces real-time close-status dashboards. It learns your process, identifies bottlenecks, and compresses timelines each cycle.

50–70%reduction in close cycle time
90%+auto-reconciliation for standard accounts
3–5 daysfreed per cycle for analysis
Near-zeromanual journal entry errors
