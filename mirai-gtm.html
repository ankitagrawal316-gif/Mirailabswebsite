01
AI SDR / Autonomous Outbound Prospecting
Your 24/7 Pipeline Engine
THE OLD WAY

SDR teams spend 70% of their time on manual prospecting — building account lists, researching contacts, writing emails, and following up. Most outbound sequences get under 2% reply rates. Hiring SDRs is expensive ($75K+ fully loaded), ramp takes 3–6 months, and turnover averages 35% annually. Competitors running AI outbound are booking meetings while your team is still building lists.

→
WITH MIRAI

AI agents run the entire outbound workflow autonomously — identify ICP-fit accounts from intent and firmographic data, find the right contacts, research each prospect, generate hyper-personalized multi-channel sequences (email, LinkedIn, phone), manage follow-up cadences, and alert human reps only when a prospect engages. Runs 24/7 at a fraction of SDR team cost.

90% of SDR workflow automated end-to-end
3–5× more meetings booked per month
70% lower cost per qualified meeting vs human SDRs
24/7 outbound running across time zones
02
AI Lead Scoring & Prioritization
Focus Reps on Deals That Close
THE OLD WAY

Sales reps waste 50%+ of their time on leads that never convert. MQLs are often unqualified, and without real-time buying signals, teams treat all leads equally. CRM lead scores are static and based on incomplete data — bloated pipelines, inaccurate forecasts, hours burned on accounts that were never going to buy.

→
WITH MIRAI

AI continuously scores every lead and account by analyzing behavioral signals (website visits, content downloads, email engagement), firmographic fit, technographic data, and third-party intent. Scores update in real time as buyer behavior changes. Reps get a dynamically ranked priority list each morning — and alerts when high-intent accounts surge.

30% improvement in lead-to-opportunity conversion
50% less time wasted on unqualified leads
20% larger average deal size from better targeting
Real-time score updates vs static weekly batches

04
Conversation Intelligence & Deal Coaching
Turn Every Call Into a Coaching Moment
THE OLD WAY

Sales managers can only listen to 1–2% of rep calls, so coaching is inconsistent and reactive. Reps repeat the same mistakes. Winning talk tracks and objection-handling live in top performers' heads and never transfer. Deals go dark and nobody knows why until it's too late.

→
WITH MIRAI

AI records, transcribes, and analyzes every sales conversation. It surfaces which talk tracks correlate with closed-won, where reps lose momentum, what objections kill deals, and when competitors are mentioned. Managers get coaching alerts and deal-risk flags; reps get real-time suggestions and post-call scorecards.

25–35% improvement in rep win rates after coaching
100% of calls analyzed vs 1–2% manually
40% faster new rep ramp time with AI-guided onboarding
Early warning deal-risk alerts before pipeline reviews
05
AI Revenue Intelligence & Adaptive Playbooks
Learn What Wins — Then Scale It
THE OLD WAY

Most teams don't know why they win or lose. Win/loss reviews happen quarterly (if at all) on anecdotal feedback. Static playbooks sit in a PDF nobody reads. Best practices stay locked in top performers' heads while 80% of the team underperforms. When the market shifts, playbooks go obsolete overnight.

→
WITH MIRAI

AI continuously mines every closed deal — CRM data, calls, email threads, proposals, competitor mentions, pricing objections, timelines — to find win/loss patterns, then auto-generates dynamic playbooks: talk tracks per persona, objection handling by competitor, stage-by-stage content, pricing guidance. Reps get contextual, in-workflow recommendations — not static documents.

20–30% increase in overall team win rates
Continuous playbook updates vs annual manual refresh
50% faster new-hire ramp to first closed deal
Data-backed every recommendation tied to deal outcomes
