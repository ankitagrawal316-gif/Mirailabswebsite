01
Autonomous Resolution Across Every Channel
One AI Agent, Chat / Email / Voice
THE OLD WAY

Support volume scales faster than headcount. Each new channel (chat, email, voice, social) needs its own staffing, and quality slips during spikes. Customers repeat themselves across channels, and 12+ minute hold times push satisfaction down and churn up.

→
WITH MIRAI

A unified AI agent resolves requests autonomously across chat, email, and voice — grounded in your product knowledge and ticket history. It understands intent, takes action (refunds, status, account changes) through your systems, and maintains one continuous context across every channel.

70%+of tickets resolved without an agent
<10 secfirst response across all channels
60% lowercost per interaction vs call center
24/7coverage in 20+ languages
02
Knowledge-Grounded Answers
Right Answer, Every Time, From Your Own Docs
THE OLD WAY

Agents hunt across wikis, PDFs, and outdated macros for answers. New hires take months to ramp. Answers vary agent to agent, and customers get confident-but-wrong information that creates repeat contacts and escalations.

→
WITH MIRAI

AI answers are grounded strictly in your approved knowledge — help center, product docs, policies, and resolved tickets. The system cites its source, refuses to guess when it lacks grounding, and surfaces stale or missing content so your team can fix the gap.

90%+answer accuracy from grounded sources

03
Clean Escalation With Full Context
Hand-offs Humans Actually Want
THE OLD WAY

When AI or tier-1 cannot resolve an issue, the customer restarts from scratch with a human. Context is lost, the customer repeats everything, and the agent spends the first five minutes catching up. Escalations feel like a punishment for both sides.

→
WITH MIRAI

When a human is needed, the AI hands off with a full summary — the issue, what's been tried, customer sentiment, account details, and a recommended next step. The human agent picks up mid-stream with everything in front of them, not a blank screen.

100%of escalations arrive with full context
5 min savedper escalated ticket on catch-up
HigherCSAT on escalated cases
Zerocustomer repetition on hand-off
